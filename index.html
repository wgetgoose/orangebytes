What
Does
Orangebytes
Do?

Orangebytes is a volunteer organization run by Carson Ferreira. We seek to help educate the elderly residents of Orange County about our new world run by technology. Sending emails, explaining what "the cloud" even is, and setting up Wi-Fi are all services we offer. If you have any other problems, just email us! We're more than happy to help. Make sure to read more below.

What we stand for.

Orangebytes is proudly non-profit.* You won't pay anything for our services!

*If there is extra hardware needed, we unfortunately are unable to cover the cost

No robots here! When you email us or leave us a voicemail, we make sure a real person responds to your request.
Orangebytes is student-run (Laguna Hills High School) and local to Orange County. We make sure to take care of you ❤️.
