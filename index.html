What
Does
Orangebytes
Do?

Orangebytes is a volunteer organization run by Carson Ferreira. We strive to help educate the elderly residents of Orange County about our new world run by technology. Sending emails, explaining what "the cloud" even is, and setting up Wi-Fi are all services we offer. If you have any other problems, just email us! We're more than happy to help. Make sure to read more below.

What we stand for

Orangebytes is proudly non-profit.* You won't pay anything for our services!

*If there is extra hardware needed, we unfortunately are unable to cover the cost

No robots here! When you email us or leave us a voicemail, we make sure a real person responds to your request.
Orangebytes is student-run (Laguna Hills High School) and local to Orange County. We make sure to take care of you ❤️.

How the process works

Step 1: Text, Call or Email Us

The first step is just to get in contact with us and describe your issue/service you want done. Don't worry if you're not entirely sure what's going on! Just describe to the best of your ability, and we'll assess and handle the rest.

Step 2: Setup a phone or video call

We will likely ask to schedule a phone or video call to get more information about your given issue. This also helps us work out a time for us to come and help solve your problem!

Step 3: Time to fix it!

Our technician will come and assess the issue on the day we schedule, and get your problem solved (yay!). Some issues might require follow-up appointments.
